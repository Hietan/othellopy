# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when needed.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project.
- Accept maintainer decisions even when a proposal is declined.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Demeaning language or repeated hostile comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or off-topic disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues or pull requests,
or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

Report concerns privately to the project maintainer. Do not use public issues
for sensitive conduct reports.
